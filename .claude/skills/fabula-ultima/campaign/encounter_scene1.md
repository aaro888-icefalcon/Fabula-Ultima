# Scene 1 encounter — "The Ambush at Sedge Ford" (Voss snatch-team)

Seeds (all honest dice): Turning Point NEW THREAD [Travel Setting · Common Social Gathering · Injustice · It's A Secret];
Q1 ExcYes (they KNOW the heir is aboard — betrayal leak); Q2 Yes (Voss-aligned, Eira's arm);
complication = Reinforcements incoming; leader role = Scholar/sage/physician.

Foes want **Odile ALIVE** (Eira's enmity: hatred+inferiority). Others: disable/deter, lethal only if pressed.

## Opening wave — 3× Voss Snatch-Scout  (reskin: Guard L5 · humanoid · soldier)
- DEX d8 INS d8 MIG d8 WLP d8 · HP 60 (Crisis 30) · MP 45 · Init 5 · DEF 11 · M.DEF **8** (weak point!)
- Heavy Spear 【DEX+MIG】 · 【HR+12】 physical  ·  Crossbow 【DEX+INS】 · 【HR+8】 physical
- Defensive Formation: +1 DEF/M.DEF while ≥2 scouts remain.  Protect: may take a hit meant for the Physic.
- Tactics: screen the Physic, box the carriage, Hinder/grab the children (nonlethal on Odile). M.DEF 8 → Odile's Ignis/Glacies shred them.

## Leader — Physic Serel, "the Quiet Hand"  (reskin: Kobold Witch L5, ELITE · humanoid)
- DEX d8 INS d8 MIG d6 WLP d10 · HP ~90 (elite) · MP 55 · Init 6 · DEF 9 · M.DEF **10**
- Oaken rod (leyline-manacle focus) 【WLP+WLP】 · 【HR+6】
- **Leyline Manacle** (10 MP · 【INS+WLP】 vs M.DEF): on hit, target gains **shaken** (WLP die ↓) AND, if target is Odile, tick Capture Clock +1.
- **Quiet Word** (Awaken-analog, 20 MP): buffs a scout's attribute die up for the scene.
- May spend from **Eira's Ultima pool (10)** for ONE dramatic power play (instant Manacle) — LOG via `fu.py points ultima -N`.
- Counterplay/weakness: no melee, fragile if the screen breaks; a SCHOLAR, not a martyr — flees with intel if the fight turns (→ mystery persists). Killing/disrupting her stalls the Capture Clock.

## Reinforcement wave — 1× Voss War-Engine  (reskin: Magitech Trooper L10 · construct)
- HP 80 (Crisis 40) · DEF 11 · M.DEF 8 · Init 5 · Axe Strike 【MIG+MIG】+1 · 【HR+14】
- Vulnerable: **air, bolt**. Immune poison. Exposed Core (Crisis): loses fire/ice resist, its axe damage becomes bolt.
- **A caged Kindred glows in its chest** — the engine's fuel. Emeric's Feral Speech / freeing it = tactical + moral lever (frees the beast, cripples the engine). Ties to canon.
- Arrives when **Reinforcement Clock [4]** fills (telegraphed: grinding gears, hot-metal glow up the road).

## Clocks (engine-tracked: `fu.py clock`)
- #1 Capture Odile [0/4] — the Quiet Hand's snatch. Fills on Manacle hits / scouts pinning Odile. FULL = Odile bound & dragged toward Voss lines (campaign-altering loss, NOT death).
- #2 Voss reinforcements [0/4] — the war-engine on the road. Telegraph before it fills; FULL = the Trooper joins.

## Kestrel's cover dilemma (the heart of the scene)
- Stay the FOOL (hold back, "lucky" flailing, Lucky Seven to explain a good hit): cover intact, but the clocks gain ground.
- Go LOUD (Bladestorm / Acceleration / Counterattack at full skill): wrecks them — but everyone present SEES the buffoon is a killing knight. Cover blown = scandal; and a surviving Voss witness (or the fleeing Physic) carries word that Duke Advain planted a blade on the heirs.

## Consequence branches (foes act to win; no soft "expected")
- Party routs them → fronts hold; Physic may still slip away with the leak → mystery thread lives.
- Capture Clock fills → Odile taken; front.eira advances hard; rescue arc.
- Party abandons the carriage/flees → survive but exposed on the road; requisition/refugee complications.
- A child downed → 0 HP → Surrender/Sacrifice fork (player's choice), Fabula economy.
