# Chaos Tendency — Fabula Ultima  (hook: chaos)

## Operative
- Start **Chaos Factor 5**; **volatility: normal** (standard Mythic chart). FU heroes are protagonists of their own story — the world pushes back hard but does not descend into pure randomness.
- Raise CF when the heroes are overwhelmed, a villain's Clock advances, or resources run dry; lower it when they seize the initiative, resolve a threat Clock, or a Bond turns the tide.
- No per-region floor by default; a besieged region or an active supreme-villain arc may set a floor of 6.
