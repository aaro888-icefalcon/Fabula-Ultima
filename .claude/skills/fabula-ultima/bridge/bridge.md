# Bridge manifest — Fabula Ultima

This companion supplies the **Fabula Ultima** ruleset (2-die attribute Checks with
d6–d12 pools, crit/fumble, HR-based damage with a VU/RS/IM/AB affinity matrix, the six
status effects, Fabula & Ultima Points, classes/skills/spells, NPC & villain design, and
a full bestiary) plus its **world-creation** session-zero. The mythic-gm engine supplies
the scene loop, oracle, honest dice, and no-softening discipline. Hooks not listed here
fall through to the engine default.

Everything mechanical lives in `data/*.json` behind the `scripts/*` CLIs; **all randomness
is delegated to `mythic-gm/scripts/dice.py` through `scripts/engine.py`** — the companion
never rolls its own dice.

**Notes for the engine/agent**
- Resolution precedence (the oracle ladder): FU rule via `fu.py` → FU generator/table → engine Fate Question. See `system-profile.md`.
- `world-tick` fires `scripts/fu.py tick <scene#>` (villain agendas, project/ritual clocks, the Fabula/Ultima economy) at every bookkeeping via `mythic-gm/scripts/tick.py <bridge> <scene#>`; see `subsystems.md`.
- `setting-canon.md` is the **live** committed world; it is generated by `scripts/worldgen.py` at session zero and grown as play travels — never hand-authored default lore.
